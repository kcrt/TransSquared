# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 2026-03-26

## Overview

TransTrans is a macOS application that provides real-time speech transcription and translation. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how TransTrans handles your data.

## Data Collection

**TransTrans does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

Specifically:

- **Audio data**: Microphone audio is processed entirely on your device using Apple's Speech framework. Audio is never recorded, stored, or sent to external servers.
- **Transcription and translation**: All speech recognition and translation are performed on-device using Apple's built-in frameworks (Speech and Translation). No text is sent to external servers.
- **Usage analytics**: TransTrans does not include any analytics, tracking, or telemetry.
- **Custom vocabulary**: Words you add to the custom vocabulary list are stored locally on your device using standard macOS preferences and are never transmitted.

## Third-Party Services

TransTrans does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs. All processing relies solely on Apple's on-device frameworks.

## Network Access

TransTrans requests network access only for downloading speech recognition language assets provided by Apple. No user data is transmitted over the network.

## Data Storage

TransTrans stores only your language preferences and custom vocabulary locally on your device using macOS UserDefaults. No data is stored in the cloud or on external servers.

## Children's Privacy

TransTrans does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the revised version will be posted here with an updated date.

## Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on the [GitHub repository](https://github.com/kcrt/TransTrans/issues).
